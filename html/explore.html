About Our Department

Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 1999. It offers one undergraduate program B.E. (CSE) with an intake of 540 students, and a postgraduate program M.Tech. (CSE) with 18 seats. With a team of well-qualified and highly motivated faculty, the department is committed to academic and research excellence. It features state-of-the-art laboratories, providing students with hands-on industry exposure. The department is NBA-accredited under the Outcome-Based Education (OBE) format and has received MODROBS funding from AICTE for setting up an IoT Lab. Additionally, it has been recognized as a Campus to Corporate Digital Academy by Capgemini, in association with ICT Academy

About Our Club

FOUNDED IN 2023, ASSOCIATION OF COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING STUDENTS SOCIETY IS THE OFFICIAL STUDENT CLUB OF THE COMPUTER SCIENCE DEPARTMENT.

The club actively organizes a variety of events, including technical workshops, hackathons, and non-technical sessions, all designed to equip members with hands-on experience and practical knowledge. By bringing together passionate and curious minds, ACSESS provides a platform to explore cutting-edge technologies, tackle real-world challenges, and develop impactful solutions. With a focus on coding, learning, and networking, ACSESS aims to empower students to grow both personally and professionally. Join us to code, connect, and make a difference!
